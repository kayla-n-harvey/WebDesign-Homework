Camping styles

  • Tent camping: pitching a tent at a park or recreational spot.
  • Backpacking: carrying a backpack with all the essentials and hiking to remote places.
  • Car camping: driving a car to a campsite and sleeping in or near the car.
  • RV/Van camping: traveling in a recreational vehicle or a van with amenities and facilities.
  • Glamping: camping in luxury with services and comforts such as beds, electricity, and bathrooms.
