Ostrich Encounter

An Ostrich encounter is likely to be a deadly one. These massive birds are able to run upto 45 mph. A single blow from an ostrich can be deadly with mortal blows the the force of their legs and lethal sharp talons. Having been known to injure or kill when provoked, coming face to face with an ostrich is no small thing.

How to Survive an attack?

When threatened by an ostrich, you become faced with 3 options.

  • Fight
  • Evade
  • Avoidance
