What to do in order to avoid a Fight with an Ostrich

  • Stay aware of your sourroundings when in their home
  • Avoid open areas
  • Avoid close contact and keep at more than 110 yard distance between you and the Ostrich
  • Never back an ostrich into a corner
  • Beware during breading season with growing herds and testostorone
  • !! Distinguish Males by their black feathers, white wingtips and a red flush on the front of their legs. Females by their brown feathers and gray wingtips and plumes !!
