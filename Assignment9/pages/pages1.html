What to do once engaged in a Fight with an Ostrich

  • Do your best to gain space to avoid the long reach of limbs
  • Use a Long Wepon or firearm if avalible
  • Aim for neck to inflict the most damage as limbs are small and easily missed, if not possible, aim for breast
  • Keep to their side or behind them as you are at most risk when face to face as they are only able to kick forward
  • If they refuse to give up, damage the wings in order to throw off its balance.
  • If given the opportunity, take a shot at their legs, this eill further damage their balance, speed and power.
  • !! Keep attention to the sharp nails on their feet, do not get struck!!
